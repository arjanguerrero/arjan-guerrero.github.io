OIKONOMICOR

Oikonomicor is a game that plays to turn a church into a computational object by augmenting its architectural space with a GUI that runs itself as a game economy.

Akin to the modern architecture that grew throughout the church, Oikonomicor overlays and re-administers da house in a way that's agnostic to its previous configurations. Over the sanctuary of the church, it also attempts transubstantiation, or transmutation, or perhaps an emergent kind of transformation.

Exhibited at Goldsmiths, University Of London, as part of the MFA Computational Arts' degree exhibition.
