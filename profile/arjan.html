Arjan Guerrero

Research Director



Arjan Guerrero is an artist and emergent researcher from Mexico City pursuing the Computational Arts MFA in Goldsmiths. In 2021-22 he pair-programmed Materias Mediales, a summer course and a series of conversations on artistic research at its intersection with contemporary techno-sciences. He currently collaborates as Creative Researcher for WYRD, a creative agency specializing in web3 and Artificial Intelligence.

Guerrero’s practice delves into technical alienation in order to investigate the latent potentials of computational technology through differential worldbuilding methodologies, often developing narratives that explore the generative capacities of epistemic media.

He has been granted by programs such as Arte Ciencia y Tecnologías (Mx), exhibited individually in artist-run spaces like Casa Maauad and collectively in other art venues like Centro Nacional de las Artes (Mexico City) and Somers Gallery (London). He received the Lumen Prize 2022 Global Majority award.
