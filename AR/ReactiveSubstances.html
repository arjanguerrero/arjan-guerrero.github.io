Reactive Substances

An ARapp for eco-sensible ARtworks

This is the exploration, taxonomization, and the making real of a New New New World of objects. By means of a series of artworks for human-sentience training and a novel image dataset for AI training (Machine Learning), we’re curating its way into existence.
As an echo of the exploration of the New World in the XV and XVI c., we are navigating the New New New World (NNNW) under a different paradigm: this is a world to be more produced than discovered, to be traced within the future more than in the past, and to be regarded as a hyperstition more than as a myth.

We're exploring the NNNW in first-hand across the Latent Space of the BigGAN—the AI image synthesizer that knows better than every other AI what the world looks like through a digital camera.

Non-hybrid class-conditional samples generated by the BigGAN (https://arxiv.org/abs/1809.11096)

To acquire such knowledge, the BigGAN was trained on ImageNet—the largest dataset for AI training, created to “map the entire world of objects”—and now is capable of generating an accurate image of any of its more-than-20-thousand types of objects. But within its mind, within its Latent Space, a series of xenobjects potentially exist; they're non-related to our human capability of imagining possible worlds and, according to owr research so far, they are all characterised in texture, structure, and situation by the particular agency of this AI model.

These xenobjects are vague in their material constitution and in their symbiotic relationships, but have a visible evolutionary chaining.

'#fossil_to_xenobject'. 2D animation that interpolates between two types of xenobjects to suggest an evolutionary path

But my main role is more like a mixture of the XVI c. Chronicler of Indies at the head of the spanish General Archive of the Indies and the heads of the ImageNet project: I’m identifying the kinds of objects that only exist within the BigGAN (the xenobjects) and organizing them as a dataset for Machine Learning (the NNNWArchive) so that the NNNW can infiltrate into the sentience of other AIs and propagate its influence on the transformations of the present material world.

Samples from the #still_architecture class of xenobjects from the NNNWArchive dataset (in process).

Just like europeans did with the New World, this Media Forensis’ research imposes an order of things over a widely unknown world. But in this case, up until now, this mediation is the only way in which the NNNW is able to shape the physical world in its image and to actually occupy the future.
